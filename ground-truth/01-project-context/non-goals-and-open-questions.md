# Non-Goals & Open Questions

## Non-goals (this phase)
- **Not a full company rebrand.** Visual identity evolves only as needed to land the new story.
- **Not replacing HubSpot.** HubSpot stays the CRM / source of truth for leads.
- **Not the externally-sold client Ground Truth product.** This Explorer is the *internal pilot
  / dogfood* over Rootstrap's own Ground Truth — the reference that makes the client product sellable.
- **Not migrating all 167 projects / 65 case studies.** Lead with a curated ICP-shaped few.
- **Not changing the commercial model on existing T&M engagements.** Delivery v2 is led-with on
  *new* opportunities; existing engagements keep their current way of working.
