# Values & Culture

> Who Rootstrap is — the identity that the **About** and **Careers** pages render and that the brand
> **voice** is built on. Public-safe; internal HR specifics (comp, performance KPIs) stay internal (BR-8).

## Core values
1. **Growth Mindset** — Learning, experimentation, and continuous improvement (including how we work with AI).
2. **Caring** — Invested in our people and our clients' success.
3. **Adaptability** — Flexible and responsive to change.
4. **Respect** — Valuing diverse perspectives and autonomy.
5. **Ownership** — Taking responsibility for outcomes, not just tasks.

## Operating principles
- **Built for adoption** — products people actually use and engage with.
- **Engineered for growth** — systems designed to scale.
- **Track. Measure. Iterate.** — continuous improvement driven by data.

## Culture (public-safe)
- **Great Place to Work** certified; senior-by-default, nearshore teams across the Americas.
- Structured mentorship, learning/conference budgets, remote-first and async-friendly, a sustainable pace.
- Recognition: Inc. 5000, Financial Times, LA Business Journal (fastest-growing), Clutch Top-Rated.

## Where this shows up
- **About** page: story + the five values. **Careers** page: values + culture + hubs.
- **Voice** (`voice-and-messaging`): the values inform tone (caring, ownership, growth — not hype).
- Internal-only: compensation, performance KPIs, tenure/turnover by role (BR-8).
