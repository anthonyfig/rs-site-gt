# Capability: Home / positioning narrative

> Human-facing unit of value. Executable detail in the user stories below.

## Purpose
In one scroll, carry a visitor from "what is Rootstrap?" to "the **AI-native capability partner that
gets AI to production**" — leading with the agentic method, using the 14-year track record as proof,
and routing ICP-fit visitors toward an AI Discovery Sprint.

## Actors
Visitor (personas A/B/C) · Marketing editor.

## Outcomes / success
- A visitor grasps what Rootstrap is and why it's different in seconds.
- The right ICP self-identifies; the primary CTA gets clicked.

## Scope
**In:** hero + value narrative, trust/proof bar, persona-aware paths, primary CTA.
**Out:** full offering detail (→ AI offering hub), case studies (→ showcase), the CRM.

## Capability-level acceptance
- [ ] Narrative leads **agentic** (Delivery v2); "nearshore agency" is not the headline (heritage = proof).
- [ ] All claims accurate: verified stats (BR-7), no internal financials (BR-8), exact Anthropic/SOC2 wording (BR-12/13).
- [ ] Persona-aware entry points; primary CTA enters the lead-capture flow.
- [ ] Server-rendered + structured data (SEO/LLM); WCAG AA; zero placeholder content.
